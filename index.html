Animal Welfare strategies for a changing world

Climate change is presenting new challenges for livestock animals. My research aims to find novel phenotypic traits to include in genetic selection programs for heat stress resiliance, also to identify new or refined animal welfare indicators that can help us to increase animal welfare without affecting production and lastly extending this strategies to smallholders and farmers around the world.

Current Work

  • Genetic selection for improved animal welfare in livestock
  • Identification of novel indicators of animal welfare
  • Genetic selection for leg health and welfare in turkeys
